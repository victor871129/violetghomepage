Frequently Asked Questions

When will you release more games?

We are working on many more games, visit this site regularly for news about new projects.

How can I report a problem inside the game?

Report bugs, request new features or suggest modifications at the email address hello@violet.guru Give us 24 hours to respond to your request and assign you a support ticket.

Why the game lose its progress?

The game can be downloaded for free and you can get to the end of the story and beyond. Progress cannot be saved unless an in-app purchase is made. It was designed that way so that the game will never have advertisements or hidden charges and by making the in-app purchase you can support the developer. That purchase is called ‹Save Progress›

How can I buy the feature ‹Save Progress›?

Click the ‹Save› button on the left side of the board and then the ‹Save Progress› button to make an in-app purchase.

How long the purchase lasts?

The purchase does not have an expiration date.

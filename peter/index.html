Frequently Asked Questions

When will you release more games?

We are working on many more games, visit this site regularly for news about new projects.

How can I report a problem inside the game?

Report bugs, request new features or suggest modifications at the email address hello@violet.guru We have a maximum capacity to respond to 1000 emails per day. Give us a maximum of 24 hours to respond to your request and assign you a support ticket.

Why the game lose its progress?

The game can be downloaded for free and if you don't close the program, you can get to the end of the story and beyond. Progress cannot be saved unless an in-app purchase is made. That purchase is called ‹Save Progress›. The game was designed that way so that it will NEVER have advertisements or hidden charges and SUPPORT many man years of development.

How can I buy the ‹Save Progress› feature?

Click the ‹save› button on the left side of the board and then click the ‹Save Progress› button.

How long the purchase lasts?

The purchase does not have an expiration date.

How can I restore the purchase?

Go to Pause menu > Leaderboard > Restore Purchases.

How to resolve the "DuplicateTransaction" message?

You'll lose the progress of the game. Go to the phone Settings > All Apps list > Peter Dreams app info > Storage > Clear Data.
